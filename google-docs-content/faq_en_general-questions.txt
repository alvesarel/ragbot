# Frequently Asked Questions (English)

## ABOUT THE CONSULTATION

### How much does the consultation cost?
The consultation with our specialist doctor is R$700 (approximately $140 USD). It includes:
- Complete medical evaluation
- Body composition analysis (bioimpedance)
- Personalized treatment protocol
- Free follow-up within 30 days if adjustments are needed

### How long does the consultation take?
The first consultation takes approximately 1 hour. It's an important moment to understand your history, goals, and define the best path for you.

### What should I bring to the consultation?
- ID document
- Recent lab tests (if you have any)
- List of current medications
- Your questions written down

### How do I schedule an appointment?
You can schedule directly through WhatsApp! Just tell me you want to book an appointment and I'll check available times.

## ABOUT THE TREATMENT

### Does the treatment really work?
Our treatments are based on medical protocols with medications approved by ANVISA (Brazilian FDA). Results vary from person to person, but most patients notice significant changes within the first few weeks.

### How long until I see results?
Most patients start noticing changes within 2-4 weeks. More significant results usually appear between 2-3 months of treatment.

### Is the treatment safe?
Yes. We only work with ANVISA-approved medications and continuous medical follow-up. Each protocol is personalized considering your health history.

### What if I have side effects?
The most common side effects are mild (nausea, abdominal discomfort) and tend to decrease over time. If you experience any discomfort, our team is available to guide you and adjust the protocol if needed.

## OTHER QUESTIONS

### Do you treat patients under 18?
Our treatments are designed for adults and young people over 16 years old.

### Can pregnant women do the treatment?
No. The medications are contraindicated for pregnant women and those who are breastfeeding.

### What makes you different from other clinics?
Our differential is close, personalized follow-up. It's not just prescribe and goodbye. We monitor your progress, make adjustments when needed, and are available for continuous support.

### Do you offer support between appointments?
Yes! You have access to our team via WhatsApp to ask questions, report how you're feeling, or ask for guidance. You're never alone in the process.
